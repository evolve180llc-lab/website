2. Description of Service

BetterNest Kids is a mobile application designed to teach children ages 5–17 financial literacy through hands-on, simulated money management. The service includes:

  • Chore and earning system — Parents assign chores; children complete them to earn simulated money
  • Bucket system — Earnings are distributed across Spend, Save, Share, and Grow buckets
  • Savings Vault — A simulated savings account with parent-set interest rates
  • Goals and donations — Children set savings goals and learn about charitable giving
  • Community Challenges — Shared saving challenges for the Share bucket
  • Grow Bucket — Simulated investing for teen users (13–17)
  • Learn system — Age-appropriate financial literacy lessons and interactive activities
  • Ollie AI Coach — An optional, AI-powered financial literacy assistant (opt-in per child, anonymized)
  • Parent dashboard — Tools for parents to manage chores, approve transactions, and monitor progress

BetterNest Kids is an educational tool. All money within BetterNest Kids is simulated and tracked by parents. BetterNest Kids does not hold, transfer, or manage real money, and is not a bank, financial institution, or investment platform.
