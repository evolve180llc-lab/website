Teach Kids Real-World Money Skills

BetterNest Kids helps children ages 5–17 earn, save, spend, share, and grow — all under parent supervision with an age-adaptive experience.

🍎 Ages 5–17 🔒 COPPA Compliant 📱 iOS & Android

How It Works

Parents set up chores and guardrails. Kids earn, manage money across smart buckets, and learn financial literacy at their own pace.

💰

Earn Through Chores

Parents assign chores with dollar amounts. Kids complete them, submit for approval, and earn money that auto-splits into buckets.

🧻

Smart Buckets

Earnings divide into Spend, Save, Share, and Grow buckets with age-appropriate ratios — building balanced money habits from the start.

🎯

Savings Goals & Vault

Kids set goals, track progress, and deposit into a Savings Vault that earns compound interest — making saving feel rewarding.

❤️

Giving & Sharing

Kids explore charitable causes, create gift funds, and join community challenges to learn the joy of giving.

📚

Financial Literacy Lessons

Age-tiered curriculum with interactive lessons, XP progression, streaks, and seven achievement levels from Money Explorer to Money Master.

🦉

Ollie — AI Money Coach

An optional AI-powered owl coach answers kids' money questions with age-appropriate guidance. All data is anonymized and stored on-device only.

Designed for Every Age

Three age tiers with progressively unlocked features ensure kids get the right experience at every stage.

🌿

Little (Ages 5–8)

Simple chores, basic money concepts, guided experience with playful encouragement.

🌱

Tween (Ages 9–12)

More autonomy, goal-setting, propose chores, and greater awareness of saving and giving.

🌳

Teen (Ages 13–17)

Full feature access including simulated investing (Grow bucket) and advanced financial concepts.

Built for Parents

You stay in control with a powerful dashboard and flexible settings for every child.

📊

Parent Dashboard

See all children's activity at a glance — pending approvals, balances, learning progress, and more.

⚙️

Flexible Controls

Set custom split ratios, interest rates, donation limits, and toggle features per child.

📈

Insights & Reports

Track chore completion, spending habits, savings trends, and giving impact over time.
