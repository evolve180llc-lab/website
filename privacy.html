Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026 · Version 1.2

Introduction

Welcome to BetterNest Kids. We take your family's privacy seriously — especially when it comes to children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have. BetterNest Kids is designed for families with children ages 5–17, and we are fully committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable privacy laws.

Please read this policy carefully. By creating a BetterNest Kids account, you agree to the practices described here. If you have questions, contact us at support@evolve-180.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Parent Account Information

When a parent creates a BetterNest Kids account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for account creation, login, and communication
  • Display name — shown within the app
  • Firebase authentication identifier (UID) — a unique ID used to secure your account
  • Consent records — timestamps and version numbers of consents you provide

1.1a Social Sign-In Data

When signing in via Google or Apple, we receive your email address and display name from the provider. We do not access your Google/Apple account data beyond authentication.

1.2 Child Profile Information

When a parent adds a child to their BetterNest Kids family, we collect:

  • Child's first name — displayed within the app
  • Date of birth — used to determine the child's age tier (Little 5–8, Tween 9–12, Teen 13–17) and provide age-appropriate content
  • Avatar selection — the emoji-based avatar the child or parent chooses
  • Age tier — automatically calculated from date of birth

1.3 Financial Activity Data

As children use BetterNest Kids, we collect and store:

  • Chore records — chore names, amounts, statuses, completion timestamps, and optional photo evidence
  • Bucket balances — current balances for Spend, Save, Share, and Grow buckets
  • Transaction history — all deposits, withdrawals, transfers, and splits across buckets
  • Savings Vault data — vault balances, deposit/withdrawal history, and interest calculations
  • Savings goals — goal names, target amounts, and progress
  • Donation records — amounts, selected causes, and timestamps
  • Allowance settings — amounts, frequency, and split ratios
  • Community Challenges — participation records and contribution amounts
  • Grow Bucket (simulated investing) — simulated investment records for teen users

1.4 Ollie AI Coach Data (Opt-In Only)

If a parent enables the Ollie AI Coach feature for their child:

  • Chat history is stored only on the local device using encrypted storage (MMKV). Chat messages are never sent to or stored on BetterNest Kids servers
  • Only the last 20 messages from the current session are retained on-device; older messages are automatically discarded
  • Chat history persists across app sessions on the device but can be cleared at any time by the parent
  • Conversation context sent to our AI provider is anonymized — no child names, dates of birth, or personally identifiable information are included

1.5 Device and Technical Data

  • Push notification device tokens (FCM) — platform type, registration timestamps
  • Security audit logs — action type, timestamp, IP address, user agent (for security monitoring only)
  • Crash reports and performance data sent to Sentry — stack traces, device info — never includes child PII
  • App Check attestation tokens (Firebase) — device integrity verification only

1.6 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use tracking cookies or web beacons
  • We do not serve third-party advertisements
  • We do not collect precise geolocation data
  • We do not collect device contacts, photos (except optional chore evidence), or microphone/camera data
  • We do not collect Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • We do not allow children to make real financial transactions or connect to bank accounts

3. How We Share Information

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

3.1 Firebase (Google) — Authentication and Analytics

We use Firebase for Authentication and anonymized analytics. Firebase does not receive any child data except anonymized, non-identifying usage events. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

3.2 Social Sign-In Providers

When you sign in with Google or Apple, your email is processed through their respective OAuth systems. See Google's privacy policy and Apple's privacy policy.

3.3 AI Provider — Ollie AI Coach Only

If a parent opts in to the Ollie AI Coach, anonymized conversation data is sent to our AI provider to generate educational responses. Before transmission, the child's name, date of birth, and any personally identifiable information are stripped. We have a data processing agreement that prohibits the provider from using conversation data to train their models.

3.4 Sentry (Error Tracking)

We use Sentry to monitor app stability and fix crashes. Sentry receives crash reports, stack traces, and device information. It does not receive child names, dates of birth, or any PII. Sentry's privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy.

3.5 No Other Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information — including children's information — to third parties. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

3.6 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or government request, or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Parental Consent (COPPA Compliance)

4.1 Consent Before Collection

BetterNest Kids requires verifiable parental consent before any child data is collected. During the onboarding process, a parent must explicitly consent to our data collection practices before adding any child profiles.

4.2 What Consent Covers

By giving consent, a parent agrees to the collection and use of their child's name, date of birth, avatar selection, financial activity data, and Ollie AI Coach conversations (if separately enabled).

4.3 Withdrawing Consent

A parent may withdraw consent at any time by deleting the child's profile from the Settings screen, contacting us at support@evolve-180.com, or deleting the entire account. Withdrawing consent will result in the permanent deletion of the child's data. This action cannot be undone.

5. Parent Rights

5.1 Review Your Child's Data

You can view all data associated with your child at any time within the app, including chore history, bucket balances, transaction records, and savings goals.

5.2 Export Your Child's Data

You can request a full export of your child's data by contacting us at support@evolve-180.com. We will provide the export within 30 days.

5.3 Delete Your Child's Data

You can delete your child's profile and all associated data from the Settings screen or by contacting us. Upon deletion, all child profile information and financial activity data are permanently removed. This action is irreversible.

5.4 Refuse Further Collection

You may refuse further collection by deleting the child's profile. The app will no longer function for that child, but your parent account and other children's profiles will not be affected.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your family's data, including encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS), encryption at rest (AES-256), Firebase Authentication with secure token-based sessions, role-based access controls, and infrastructure running on Google Cloud with SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certifications. Children do not have separate login credentials, reducing the attack surface.

While we take extensive measures to protect data, no system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach, we will notify you promptly as required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

BetterNest Kids is designed specifically for use by children under parental supervision. We are committed to COPPA compliance: children do not create their own accounts, parental consent is required before collecting any child data, we collect only data necessary for the educational experience, we never use children's data for targeted advertising, community features do not expose children's identities, AI interactions are anonymized, and parents have full visibility and control over all child data and features.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date, increment the version number, and for material changes, notify parents via email or in-app notice. For changes affecting children's data practices, we will request renewed parental consent.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your parental rights, or have concerns about how we handle your family's data, contact us at support@evolve-180.com.

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.

11. California Residents

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt out. We do not sell personal information. Contact us at support@evolve-180.com to exercise your rights.

12. International Users

BetterNest Kids is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the service from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and stored in the United States. By using BetterNest Kids, you consent to this transfer.
